About Me

Welcome Wanderer!

My name is Denny and I am a rising senior studying computer engineering and attending the University of Houston. I have been a Software Development Intern at Liberty Mutual, handling Salesforce and Selenium test suites and at Satellite Tracking of People LLC where I built the front end of our company Android App and ran regression tests on our websites. I have experience speaking with people as I worked at Micro Center as a Sales Associate and was able to hold a 93% satisfaction record. Other ways I have improved my people skills and professionalism is through holding leadership roles as Historian and Webmaster for the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers - UH Chapter, Arrangement Chair for the Student Advisory Committee, and Resource Manager for the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. I have experience in other areas such as retail and leadership roles that have allowed for me to improve my professionalism and speaking skills.

My interests are to work with AI, cars, and emerging technology no matter where it is. My goals in life are to be able to save the planet, help children, adopt dogs, and most importantly... Never stop getting better.
